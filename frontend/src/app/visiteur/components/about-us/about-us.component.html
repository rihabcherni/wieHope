Wie affinity group

WIE, an acronym for Women in Engineering, represents a vibrant community within the IEEE that serves as a beacon for diversity, inclusion, and innovation in the engineering realm. our affinity group dedicated to empowering women engineers, scientists, academia, and industry professionals across diverse engineering fields. As an integral part of the IEEE, our Women in Engineering (WIE) community sets the gold standard for fostering diversity, inclusion, and innovation in the engineering realm.

Wie hope

Welcome to WIE Hope, a dynamic web application empowered by the IEEE ENSIT WIE Affinity Group. WIE Hope is more than just an innovative platform; it represents our commitment to making a positive impact, guided by the core values of the IEEE ENSIT WIE Affinity Group. Our application is crafted by a team of young engineers driven by the desire to engage in meaningful and positive actions. We are dedicated to inspiring others to join us in empowering young students, helping them rise above the challenges of poverty, and ensuring they navigate their school years under improved conditions. Together we build a future where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to the positive transformation of our world.
